About Me

As someone who has a background in fashion you may wonder how I got into web development. Well the short story is I wanted to something that I found to be interesting and challenging at the same time and web development was just that. With web development I have learned an array of things that have facilitated my growth in a way I could have never imagined. While still I lust after fashion as one does, I am now able to incorporate my passions into something that is far more tangible than working within the field itself. Alongside my background I find interests in photography and cooking.
